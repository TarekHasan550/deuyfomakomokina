Got Questions?
We’ve Got Answers!

How do I know you won’t trap me in long-term contracts?

We don’t believe in locking clients into long-term contracts. You own everything – your website, your domain, and your hosting. We make sure you have full access and control from day one. If you ever decide to leave, you can take your website with you, no hidden fees, no surprises.

Are you going to overcharge me with hidden fees?

We believe in transparent pricing. Before starting, we provide a clear breakdown of costs so you know exactly what you're paying for. No last-minute surprises. If a service isn't essential, we won’t push it.

Will you disappear after delivering the website?

We don’t just build websites – we build long-term relationships. That’s why we offer post-launch support and training to help you manage your website. If you ever need help, we’ll be here.

Are you using cheap templates and selling them as ‘custom’ websites?

Every business is unique, and so is every website we build. We tailor each design to fit your brand, your audience, and your goals. We don’t just reskin templates – we create solutions that actually help you grow.

Can you show proof of past work and real client references?

Fubiweb is new, but our expertise isn’t. Our team has built multiple projects before launching this agency. While we don’t have a long client list yet, we’re confident in our work. We can show you sample projects, offer a test project, or even build a free concept so you can see our quality firsthand.

How do I know you won’t vanish if I need future updates?

We’re building Fubiweb for the long haul, and our goal is to become your trusted digital partner. We offer ongoing support, and you’ll always have a way to reach us.

Why should I trust a new agency like Fubiweb?

We may be new, but our skills, experience, and commitment to results speak louder. We bring fresh ideas, personalized attention, and a hunger to prove our worth. Plus, we offer flexible pricing and a risk-free process to build trust with you.

Your pricing seems too low - is this a scam?

We understand why you’d ask that. Many agencies overcharge, so when they see fair pricing, it feels 'too good to be true.' Our approach is simple – we price fairly based on the work needed, without unnecessary markups. And if you want premium custom solutions, we provide those too.

How can I verify your testimonials and case studies?

Since we’re new, we’re actively collecting testimonials and case studies. If you'd like, we can connect you with one of our early clients, or you can check real projects we’ve worked on.

Can you guarantee results?

We guarantee effort, strategy, and a website designed for results. But like any business tool, a website’s success depends on factors like marketing, content, and audience engagement. What we do guarantee is a solid foundation that supports your goals and maximizes your chances of success.

How much does it cost?

Our pricing depends on your specific needs. Instead of offering a one-size-fits-all price, we tailor our solutions based on your goals and budget. Let’s jump on a quick call so we can understand what you need and provide a clear quote.

Can I just use Wix or WordPress myself?

Absolutely! DIY platforms like Wix and WordPress are great for simple websites. However, businesses that want a strong brand presence, better conversions, and a site that scales usually prefer a custom-built website. It all depends on what’s best for your goals – would you like me to guide you on the pros and cons?

What if I don’t like the final design?

That’s a valid concern, and that’s why we involve you throughout the process. Before we finalize anything, we present concepts and revisions based on your feedback. Our goal is to make sure you're 100% happy with the end result.

How long will it take?

Timelines depend on the project’s complexity. A simple website might take a few weeks, while a larger one could take longer. We prioritize quality over rushing, but we also make sure to meet deadlines. If you have a target launch date, we can plan accordingly.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Yes! We build websites with user-friendliness in mind. You’ll have full control, and we provide training or guides to help you manage updates. Of course, if you ever need extra support, we’re here to help.

What happens if I need changes after the project is finished?

We offer different support options after launch, from one-time updates to ongoing maintenance plans. You’ll always have access to your site, so you’re never locked in – but if you ever need help, we’re here.

What if I change my mind after starting the project?

We get it – priorities change. That’s why we break the project into phases, so you’re not committing to everything upfront. If something comes up, we’ll work with you to adjust.
